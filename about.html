About Us

"The" School

"Delivering the best...from initiation to intellect "

Koshal Public School was established in the year 2023 with the moto of providing good quality education from the very tender age of a child. With our modern approach of teaching, we have been able to teach the young ones the way they need to be. The school's home-like atmosphere and motherly love of the teachers, has brought about parents saying that their children are always willing to go to the school instead of complaining and making excuses not to.

Our teaching methodology inculcates the spirit of excellence in all spheres of activities. The children get the right mix of fun packed learning experience with the emphasis on individual development that relieves the stress of education and triggers happy learning. The teaching environment provides the knowledge for physical, intellectual, aesthetic, emotional, spiritual and social development of the child. Our main objective is to provide good quality education with all infrastructural facilities.

Mrs.Sumansudha Tripathy,Principal

The Principal Mrs.Sumansudha Tripathy nurtures every child with her motherly love and tenderly care. According to her "The learning process is not hush-hush, but the child is encouraged to speak and interact as much as possible."

"The"Pillars

The Two Pillars to success

Our Vision

To bring an all round balanced development in the personality of the child and contribute to the physical welfare of the children, and taking our country to a next level of Growth...

Our Mission

To develop young ones with Knowledge, Intelligence as well as Creativity, which gives them the confidence and courage to think "OUT OF THE BOX"......and to create a Child Friendly environment where the children can learn various activities without any hesitation.
